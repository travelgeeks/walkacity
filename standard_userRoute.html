Help

Welcome to the Walk-a-City help section!

Please use the "Help" link on the top to help you navigate and explore thorugh this app.

  • Standard route

      Standard routes (see below) are routes that are recommended by Walk-a-City. This button will bring you to routes that you will look forward to! So do keep a look out for more updates on the new routes!

  • Users' route

      User uploaded (see below) routes are routes that are recommend by Walk-a-City users. What's more? You can also upload a new route by clicking on Users' Routes -> Upload Route to share your custom route with other users!
