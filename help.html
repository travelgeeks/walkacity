Navigation around the app

  1. Select the appropriate city and the country you wish to see.
  2. Choose either standard route or user uploaded routes. (Standard routes are routes that are recommended by Walk-a-City whereas User uploaded routes are routes that are recommend by users)
  3. Follow the map and have an awesome experience in the city!
