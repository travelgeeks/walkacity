Rue des Marronniers

The Rue des Marronniers is a street located in the Bellecour quarter, in the 2nd arrondissement of Lyon. It is a small paved pedestrian street famous for its many bouchons. It is served by the metro station Bellecour and many buses. The street belongs to a zone classified as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO. Bouchons you should not miss out include Chez Mounier (value-for-money food)and Café des Fédérations, one of the oldest Bouchons in Lyon and also one of the best.
