Halles de Lyon-Paul Bocuse

Halles de Lyon-Paul Bocuse is a covered market comprising more than sixty traders. It is one of the centre of gastronomy for Lyon, and is also known as the 'Belly of Lyon'. In 2006, following renovations and to pay tribute to Paul Bocuse, the most famous chef in Lyon, Les Halles were renamed 'Les Halles de Lyon Paul Bocuse." Many of the restaurants here are star-rated by the Michelin guide. Discover bakers, caterers, butchers or fishmongers selling high-quality fresh products.
