Help

Welcome to the Walk-a-City help section!

Please use the "Help" link on the top to help you navigate and explore through this app.

  • View Map

      View Map with street view lets you explore places suggested by users. You can explore world landmarks, view landmarks, navigate a trip, and an array of photos in impressive descriptions. Browse the photo gallery (bottom of the page) to see collections from around the world! Click on more to find out more to see a full description of the landmark!

  • Rate & Review

      Users can view a list of ratings submitted by other users based on their own personal experiences and you can also provide a review of the route if you have something to share too!
