How to upload a new route?

  1. Step 1: Start by selecting a point on the map.
  2. Step 2: Enter the name of the point in the unput box below.
  3. Step 3: Click the Add button to record the point selected.
  4. Step 4: Repeat Step 1 to 3 as many times as necessary.
  5. Step 5: Enter a route description and submit.
