Welcome to the Walk-a-City help section!

Please use the "Help" link on the top to help you navigate and explore through this app.

How to upload a new route?

  1. Click on Upload New Route and you will be directed to a Upload Route page.
  2. Enter the required details - name of route.
  3. Click on Submit button to add a new route.
  4. Congratulations! You have successfully added a new route. Your new route will appear at the top of the list of routes page.
